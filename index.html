Walking the Path Together

Theravada Buddha Sasana Organization (TBSO) is located in the North East of Seattle, Washington State. We are a monastery in the Dhamma Gone Yee Mahasi Sasana Yeikthar of Seattle Myanmar Buddhist organization. TBSO was founded in September of 2009. Our mission is to promote and teach Buddha's teachings and cultures. TBSO is essentially just beginning; it is a young monastery with great growth potential.

All donations go to support our Buddha Mission - TBSO(USA) to establish a meditation center for your present and beyond the life goal. Donations, as always, are very much appreciated. Many thanks for your kind donation such as New Dhamma Hall donation, monthly donation, daily breakfast and lunch donation, monthly four requisites and etc.

May all of you grow longevity, beauty, happiness and strength in physical and mental by the power of wholesomeness.

"Peace comes from within. Do not seek it without."

Siddhartha Gautama
