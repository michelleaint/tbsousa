Burmese New Year Complimentary Food Fair

On 30th May 2026 (Saturday) Theravada Buddha Sasana Organization (TBSO) will be celebrating "Burmese New Year Complimentary Food Fair" according to generosity of Burmese New Year culture and water festival with "Classic Boys" Live Band. All of you are cordially invited to participate in enjoying delicious various kinds of Food with friends on that day. Many thanks for your kind supporting us.

DATE 30th May 2026 (Saturday) TIME
12:00 PM - 3:00 PM
PLACE 17730 Broadway Ave
Snohomish, WA 98296 TEL (360) 243-3468
